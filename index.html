Your Journey

The Home Buying Process

A step-by-step guide to buying your dream home in the USA — from financial preparation all the way to getting your keys.

01
🤝
Meet Your Agent
Day 1

A great agent is your biggest asset. I'll listen to your needs, understand your timeline, and build a personalised strategy just for you.

  • Schedule a free consultation with me
  • Define must-haves vs. nice-to-haves
  • Set realistic market expectations
  • Sign buyer's representation agreement
02
💳
Prepare Finances
Weeks 1 – 2

Set yourself up for success before touring homes. Lenders want to see financial stability and a clear picture of your income.

  • Review & improve your credit score (aim 620+)
  • Calculate your budget & down payment (3–20%)
  • Gather W-2s, tax returns & bank statements
  • Pay down outstanding debts where possible
03
✅
Get Pre-Approved
Week 2

Pre-approval gives you a clear budget and shows sellers you're a serious buyer. I'll connect you with trusted lenders to get this done fast.

  • Choose a lender (I can recommend trusted ones)
  • Submit mortgage application & documents
  • Receive pre-approval letter with loan amount
  • Explore down payment assistance programs
04
🏠
Start Home Shopping
Weeks 2 – 8

The exciting part. Staying focused on your criteria saves time and avoids decision fatigue. I'll send curated matches straight to you.

  • Receive curated MLS listings daily
  • Tour homes in person & virtually
  • Research neighborhoods, schools & amenities
  • Track & compare your shortlisted homes
05
📝
Make an Offer
1 – 3 Days

Found the one? I'll analyse comparable sales to craft a competitive offer that protects your interests and stands out to the seller.

  • Comparative market analysis (comps)
  • Submit earnest money deposit (1–3%)
  • Include smart contingency clauses
  • Present offer with a personal touch
06
🔍
Order an Inspection
Within 7 – 10 Days

Once your offer is accepted, a professional inspection protects you from costly hidden surprises before you're fully committed.

  • Hire a certified home inspector
  • Attend the inspection in person
  • Review the full inspection report
  • Identify any issues requiring attention
07
🤝
Negotiate Final Offer
2 – 5 Days

Based on inspection findings, I'll negotiate repairs, credits, or price reductions on your behalf to ensure you get the best possible deal.

  • Request repairs or seller concessions
  • Negotiate closing cost contributions
  • Agree on final purchase price
  • Sign the final purchase agreement
08
🏦
Appraisal Ordered
Weeks 3 – 5

Your lender orders an independent appraisal to confirm the home's market value. Staying responsive during underwriting speeds the process.

  • Lender schedules independent appraiser
  • Respond promptly to underwriter requests
  • Lock in your interest rate
  • Receive "Clear to Close" approval
09
🗝️
Closing Day
The Big Day

The finish line. Bring your ID and certified funds. Everything else, I'll have already taken care of.

  • Final walkthrough of the property
  • Review & sign all closing documents
  • Pay closing costs & down payment balance
  • Receive the keys to your new home!
10
🚚
Schedule Your Move
You're Home!

Congratulations! My support doesn't end at closing. I'll connect you with trusted movers, contractors, and local resources to settle in smoothly.

  • Book a reputable moving company early
  • Transfer utilities & update your address
  • Get recommendations for local contractors
  • Enjoy your new home!
Sell With Confidence

The Home Selling Process

A step-by-step guide to selling your home for the best price — from preparation and pricing through to collecting your proceeds.

01
🤝
Choose an Agent
Day 1

Choosing the right agent makes all the difference. I'll walk you through my proven process, set clear expectations, and build a strategy tailored to your home and goals.

  • Schedule a free seller consultation
  • Review my marketing approach
  • Discuss your timeline & priorities
  • Ask questions, no pressure, no obligation
02
📊
Market Analysis
Week 1

I run a detailed Comparative Market Analysis using recent local sales to understand exactly where your home stands in today's market.

  • Review comparable recent sales (comps)
  • Analyse active & pending listings
  • Assess your home's unique features
  • Identify the strongest pricing window
03
💲
Establish a Sales Price
Week 1

Pricing correctly from day one is critical. Overpriced homes sit unsold; underpriced homes leave money on the table. I'll find the sweet spot that maximises your return.

  • Set a data-driven listing price
  • Balance speed of sale vs. maximum return
  • Factor in buyer psychology & market timing
  • Agree on a pricing strategy going forward
04
✍️
Sign Listing Agreement
Week 1 – 2

Once we've agreed on price and strategy, we formalise our partnership. I'll walk you through every clause so you sign with full confidence.

  • Review listing agreement terms
  • Confirm listing duration & commission
  • Agree on showing preferences & lockbox setup
  • Sign & officially go to market
05
📸
Marketing Campaign
Week 2 – 3

Maximum exposure means maximum offers. Your home will be seen by thousands of qualified buyers across every major platform.

  • Professional photography & video walkthrough
  • MLS listing live on 900+ platforms
  • Targeted social media & digital ad campaigns
  • Hosted open houses for maximum foot traffic
06
🤜
Negotiate & Accept Offer
1 – 3 Days

The best offer isn't always the highest number. I'll walk you through every detail so you choose the offer that truly serves your goals.

  • Compare all offers side by side
  • Evaluate price, contingencies & timeline
  • Counter-offer or accept with expert guidance
  • Negotiate repairs, credits & closing costs
07
🔍
Home Inspection
Within 7 – 10 Days

Once under contract, the buyer orders a professional inspection. I'll protect your position during negotiations and ensure repair requests are fair and reasonable.

  • Buyer schedules certified home inspector
  • Review inspection report findings
  • I negotiate any repair requests on your behalf
  • Agree on credits or repairs to keep deal on track
08
🏦
Home Appraisal
Weeks 3 – 4

The buyer's lender orders an independent appraisal to verify the home's value. If it comes in low, I'll negotiate to protect your sale price.

  • Lender-ordered independent appraisal
  • I provide the appraiser with supporting comps
  • Address any value gaps with the buyer's lender
  • Confirm appraisal clears for financing
09
⚖️
Title Commitment
Weeks 4 – 5

A title company searches public records to confirm you have clear ownership. Any issues found are resolved before closing to protect both parties.

  • Title company conducts full title search
  • Resolve any liens, judgments, or title issues
  • Issue title commitment to buyer's lender
  • Confirm all contingencies are satisfied
10
🗝️
Closing Day
The Big Day

The moment you've been working toward. I'll be with you every step of the way. Sign, hand over the keys, and enjoy your proceeds.

  • Buyer completes final walkthrough
  • Sign all transfer & closing documents
  • Receive net proceeds via wire transfer
  • Hand over the keys, congratulations!
