Your Journey

The Home Buying Process

A step-by-step guide to buying your dream home in the USA — from financial preparation all the way to getting your keys.

💳
Financial Preparation
Weeks 1 – 2

Set yourself up for success before touring homes. Lenders want to see financial stability and a clear picture of your income.

  • Review & improve your credit score (aim 620+)
  • Calculate budget & down payment (3–20%)
  • Gather W-2s, tax returns & bank statements
  • Get mortgage pre-approval from a lender
1
Prepare
🤝
Meet Your Agent
Week 1

A great agent is your biggest asset. Pooja will listen to your needs, understand your timeline, and build a personalised strategy.

  • Schedule a free consultation
  • Define must-haves vs. nice-to-haves
  • Set realistic market expectations
  • Sign buyer's representation agreement
2
Engage
🏠
Home Search
Weeks 2 – 8

The exciting part — but staying focused on your criteria saves time and avoids decision fatigue.

  • Receive curated MLS listings daily
  • Tour homes in person & virtually
  • Research neighborhoods, schools & amenities
  • Track & compare your shortlisted homes
3
Search
📝
Make an Offer
1 – 3 Days

Pooja analyses comparable sales to craft a competitive offer that protects your interests and stands out to the seller.

  • Comparative market analysis (comps)
  • Submit earnest money deposit (1–3%)
  • Include smart contingency clauses
  • Negotiate counteroffers confidently
4
Offer
🔍
Under Contract
Weeks 1 – 3

Once your offer is accepted, due diligence begins. This phase protects you from costly hidden surprises.

  • Professional home inspection ($300–$500)
  • Review report & request repairs or credits
  • Lender-ordered independent appraisal
  • Title search & purchase title insurance
5
Due Diligence
🏦
Mortgage Approval
Weeks 3 – 5

Your lender's underwriting team reviews everything. Staying responsive and avoiding new credit inquiries speeds this up significantly.

  • Submit full mortgage application
  • Respond promptly to underwriter requests
  • Lock in your interest rate
  • Receive "Clear to Close" approval
6
Financing
🗝️
Closing Day
The Big Day

The finish line. Bring your ID and certified funds — everything else, Pooja has already taken care of.

  • Final walkthrough of the property
  • Review & sign all closing documents
  • Pay closing costs & down payment balance
  • Receive the keys to your new home!
7
Close
Sell With Confidence

The Home Selling Process

A step-by-step guide to selling your home for the best price — from preparation and pricing through to collecting your proceeds.

🧹
Prepare Your Home
Weeks 1 – 2

First impressions sell homes. Buyers decide within seconds — a clean, well-presented property commands a higher price and sells faster.

  • Declutter, depersonalise & deep clean
  • Complete minor repairs & fresh paint
  • Boost curb appeal — lawn, landscaping, entry
  • Professional staging consultation
1
Prepare
📊
Price & Strategy
Weeks 1 – 2

Pricing correctly from day one is critical. Overpriced homes sit unsold; underpriced homes leave money on the table. Pooja finds the sweet spot.

  • Free comparative market analysis (CMA)
  • Review recent comparable sales
  • Set a competitive listing price
  • Agree on marketing strategy & timeline
2
Strategy
📸
List & Market
Week 2 – 3

Maximum exposure means maximum offers. Your home will be seen by thousands of qualified buyers across every major platform.

  • Professional photography & video walkthrough
  • MLS listing live on 100+ platforms
  • Targeted social media & digital ad campaigns
  • Feature on Dream Homes website & print materials
3
Market
🚪
Showings & Open Houses
Weeks 2 – 6

Every showing is an opportunity. Pooja handles all scheduling and feedback so you can focus on your daily life with minimal disruption.

  • Flexible scheduling around your routine
  • Hosted open houses for maximum exposure
  • Real-time showing feedback & reports
  • Strategy adjustments based on market response
4
Showings
📋
Review Offers
1 – 3 Days

The best offer isn't always the highest number. Pooja walks you through every detail so you choose the offer that truly serves your goals.

  • Compare all offers side by side
  • Evaluate price, contingencies & timeline
  • Counter-offer or accept with expert guidance
  • Negotiate repairs, credits & closing costs
5
Offers
🔎
Under Contract
Weeks 1 – 3

Once you accept an offer, the buyer conducts due diligence. Pooja protects your position at every step and keeps the deal on track.

  • Buyer's home inspection — Pooja negotiates repair requests
  • Lender-ordered appraisal (buyer's side)
  • Title search & escrow account opened
  • Satisfy remaining contract contingencies
6
Contract
💰
Closing Day
The Big Day

The moment you've been working toward. Pooja is with you every step of the way — sign, hand over the keys, and enjoy your proceeds.

  • Buyer completes final walkthrough
  • Sign all transfer & closing documents
  • Receive net proceeds via wire transfer
  • Hand over the keys — congratulations!
7
